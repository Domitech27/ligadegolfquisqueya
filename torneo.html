Summer Contest

Enter for a chance to win a coaching session with PGA Canada's #1 Ranked Pro Golfer, Dave Levesque!

This Summer we're awarding two Shadow Mountain golfers with a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to improve their golf skills and learn from the best! The winners will join Canadian Golf Sensation Dave Levesque for an afternoon of golf during 'Labour Day' weekend.

To participate, keep an eye out for the special 'Titleist Summer Contest' flag which will be staged at different holes throughout the months of June, July and August. Enter your 'Par vs Strokes' tally at the hole and submit!

The more you play, the better your odds. Submit your score during each game to increase your chances of winning!
